Interstellar Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

Last Updated: April 19th, 2024.

Section 1: Definitions

In this privacy policy, "We" refers to "Canvas Timeline®". "Personally-identifiable information" (PII) refers to information that can be used to identify a person such as IP addresses. "Official Canvas Timeline® deployments" refer to the Canvas Timeline® application running on the official server. "Canvas Timeline® deployment operator" refers to the person(s) running the Canvas Timeline® application. "ToS" refers to Terms of Service.

Please redirect any general inquiries to our official community on support page.

Section 2: Summary

  • By using Canvas Timeline®, you agree you understand and acknowledge this privacy policy and its clauses. This page has been created to help you understand what types of data we collect, how we use and keep your data safe, etc. If you do not agree with this privacy policy and terms of service, you must cease use of our services.

Section 2.1: Summary - Offical Canvas Timeline® Deployment

Official Canvas Timeline® deployments are run with privacy in mind. We do temporarily collect and retain some PII such as IP addresses used to access the official Canvas Timeline® deployment, but this is not intentional.

Section 2.2: Summary - Unofficial Canvas Timeline® Deployments

  • Canvas Timeline® developers are commited to ensuring Canvas Timeline® remains as a secure application. However, we cannot guarantee the same level of security or privacy of unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployments.
  • The type of information collected on community deployments is completely dependent on who and where the deployment is being hosted.
  • Generally, we recommend Canvas Timeline® deployment operators to collect as minimal information as possible.
  • We cannot control unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployments.
  • We cannot verify the authenticity of the code in unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployments. This is an extremely rare situation, but we will not be held responsible if your data such as an account becomes compromised as a result of malicious code in an unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployment.

Section 4: The information we collect

Section 4.1: The official Canvas Timeline® deployment

  • Information that may be unintentionally collected as part of using Canvas Timeline®'s official deployment may include basic information such as your IP address, web traffic when using Canvas Timeline®, etc. This information is temporary and is kept until the server is rebooted.
  • We do not purposefully collect anyone's information. We try to limit the data collected by the official Canvas Timeline® deployment.
  • We do not, will not, and will never knowingly collect the information of minors under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by laws in your country).

Section 4.2: Unofficial Canvas Timeline® Deployments

  • We can't give a definitive answer on what information is collected when you use a unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployment, only the operator of the Canvas Timeline® deployment you're using can.
  • We urge operators to collect and store as minimal information as possible, but we can't control what they do.

Section 5: Control

  • We do not have the power to control what unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployment operators do with their deployment.
  • We protect information stored on the official Canvas Timeline® deployment by limiting access. The only person that has access to the server of which the official Canvas Timeline® deployment runs on is the server host and the owner of Canvas Timeline®, xbubbo.
  • We cannot check every deployment for malicious code. If any data such as your account gets compromised after you access it on a unofficial Canvas Timeline® deployment, we will not be held liable.

Section 6: How we use basic information on the official Canvas Timeline® deployment that may be collected accidentally

  • Your IP address is used to allow you to use the Canvas Timeline® service. This is how it works for every website you visit, not just Canvas Timeline®. The difference is whether or not this data is intentionally collected and stored.
  • Info about your web traffic while using Canvas Timeline® is needed to allow you to access the service you wish to access.
  • Canvas Timeline® will NEVER sell your info to third-parties, other than to some ad companies. We do this so we can make enough money to pay for our server.
